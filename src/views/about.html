About

The goal of this game is to provide an easy way to just pickup and learn new words. As a child I had the pleasure of winning an electronic dictionary that I would sit for hours playing its built-in games such as hangman, anagrams, etc. Yet I always wished it had a way to show a random word and I guess the defintion. This app is a fruitition of that youthful desire that I wish to share with the world.
